## A dateset from an historical collection 
The lichen collection "Abramo Massalongo" includes 594 exsiccata of 490 taxa, mainly collected in North-Eastern Italy between 1845 and 1855. The collection represents a historically and taxonomically significant dataset documenting early lichenological research in Italy and Europe.

### Availability
The dataset is available at <a href="https://doi.org/10.15468/6gcus5" target="_blank">https://doi.org/10.15468/6gcus5</a>
