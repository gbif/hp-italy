## Two large datesets of marine amphipod species 
The University of Palermo just published two datasets of marine amphipod species for Europe (18559 occurrence records) and Italy (4332 occurrence records). The two datasets originate from literature surveys as well as from previously unpublished data. 

### Availability
The dataset <i>Amphipod species in European marine waters</i> is available at <a href="https://doi.org/10.15468/bac3bs" target="_blank">https://doi.org/10.15468/bac3bs</a><br><br>
The dataset <i>Inventory of marine amphipod species from Italian waters: an update</i> is available at <a href="https://doi.org/10.15468/2u8gud" target="_blank">https://doi.org/10.15468/2u8gud</a>
